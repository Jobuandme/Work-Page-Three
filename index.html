Yearbook Designs

During my grade 12 year in high school I created and led our yearbook design team. I was responsible for
creating the majority of the graphics seen in the book as well as doing the lay out for the pages. Seen below
is the cover as well as some of the section titles from the book.

My experience leading the yearbook team gave me excellent practice for creating a theme, and sticking to it.
We had to come up with a theme and style, and keep it consistent throughout the book. The book was
created by a team of people with different perspectives on design. To help my team follow the theme and
style I created a standardized background for every page, designated fonts and colours, and created various
icons for use throughout the book. Establishing the identity for the book is the main factor in what made it
cohesive and enjoyable for every student to read.
Designs made in Photoshop.
